FAQs: Common Air Duct & Dryer Vent Cleaning Questions

1. How often should air ducts be cleaned?

Air ducts should typically be cleaned every 3 to 5 years, depending on factors like pets, allergies, renovations, and overall air quality. Regular cleaning helps improve HVAC efficiency and indoor air quality.

2. Why is dryer vent cleaning important?

Dryer vent cleaning is crucial for preventing fire hazards caused by lint buildup. It also improves dryer efficiency, reduces energy costs, and extends the lifespan of your appliance.

3. What are the signs my air ducts need cleaning?

Common signs include visible dust around vents, increased allergy symptoms, musty odors, reduced airflow, and higher energy bills. If you notice any of these, it's time to schedule an air duct cleaning service.

4. Do you offer commercial air duct cleaning?

Yes, we specialize in commercial air duct cleaning for offices, retail spaces, and facilities. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption while ensuring a clean and healthy work environment.

5. Are your cleaning methods safe and eco-friendly?

Absolutely. We use eco-friendly cleaning solutions and state-of-the-art equipment that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment while delivering thorough results.

6. How long does the cleaning process take?

Typical air duct or dryer vent cleaning takes 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of the system and level of buildup. Our technicians work efficiently to complete the job with minimal disruption.
